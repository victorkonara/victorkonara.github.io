Victor Konara is a Sri Lankan author. He lives on a mountain near Kandy, attempting to broker a lasting peace between twelve monkeys, two deer, and one wild boar. In his youth he attempted to take over the world with an Army of Darkness (read: goats with AK-47s); his mind shattered and he spent many years wandering the wilderness. He eventually stumbled upon the world of Singhapura and Daub, and now spends his time picking cat hair out of his clothes. 

He also writes as Yudhanjaya Wijeratne.
